\begin{align}

\end{align}
Note: Cross product is given by:
\begin{align}
\label{eq:solutions/point_vector/17 3}
\myvec{a_1\\a_2\\a_3}\times

\myvec{0&-a_3&a_2\\a_3&0&-a_1\\-a_2&a_1&0}
\myvec{b_1\\b_2\\b_3}
\end{align}
