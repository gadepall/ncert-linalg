Let 
% 
\begin{align}
\frac{3x-4}{2} = \frac{x+1}{4}-1 + s,  \quad s \geq 0
\end{align}
Then, 
\begin{align}
5x -5 -4s = 0 
\\
\implies x=1+\frac{4s}{5}
\\
\implies x \geq 1
\end{align}
