Given determinant:
\mydet{x & a & x+a\\
y & b & y+b\\
z &c &z+c}=0\\
Applying transformation:\\
\begin{align}
\mydet{x & a & x+a\\
y & b & y+b\\
z &c &z+c}
\xleftrightarrow[]{C_3 \leftarrow C_3- C_2 } 
&
\mydet{x & a & x\\
y & b & y\\
z &c &z}
\end{align}
\begin{align}
\mydet{x & a & x\\
y & b & y\\
z &c &z}
\xleftrightarrow[]{C_3 \leftarrow C_3- C_1} 
&
\mydet{x & a & 0\\
y & b & 0\\
z &c &0}
\label{kval}
\end{align}
From \ref{kval} If any row or column of determinant is zero ,than it's value is zero
\begin{align}
\mydet{x & a & x+a\\
y & b & y+b\\
z &c &z+c}
=
\mydet{x & a & 0\\
y & b & 0\\
z &c &0}=0
\end{align}
