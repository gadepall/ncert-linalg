	
Let x be an odd natural number and y be the odd natural number consecutive to x.
	\begin{align}
	\therefore y=x+2
	\end{align}
	We need to find x and y  such that 
	\begin{multline}
x,y >10 \text{ and } x+y<40\\
\therefore x+x+2<40\\
2x+2<40\\
x+1<20\\
x<19
	\end{multline}
	
	
	Hence the condition is satisfied when $x>10$ and $x<19$
