The normal vector of the perpendicular line is
\begin{equation}
\begin{pmatrix}
7 \\
1
\end{pmatrix}       
\end{equation}
Thus, the desired equation of the line is 
\[
\begin{pmatrix}
7 & 1
\end{pmatrix}
\begin{pmatrix}
\vec{x} - 
\begin{pmatrix}
3 \\
0
\end{pmatrix}
\end{pmatrix} 
= 0
\]
\[
\Longrightarrow 
\begin{pmatrix}
7 & 1
\end{pmatrix}
\vec{x}
= 21
\]
