\begin{align}
\vec{A}&= \myvec{158 & -378 \\ -378 & 152} \\
\vec{b}&= \myvec{-74 \\ -604} \\
\vec{R}&= \myvec{  0 & -1 \\ 1 & 0} \\
\vec{R}\vec{A}\vec{R}\vec{A}\vec{x}&= \vec{R}\vec{A}\vec{R}\vec{b} \label{eq:solutions/line_plane/231}\\
\vec{R}\vec{A}\vec{R}\vec{A}&= k \vec{I}
\end{align}
I is the Identity Matrix and k is the constant which is equal to 118868 which is shown below
\begin{align}
k\vec{I}\vec{x}&= \vec{R}\vec{A}\vec{R}\vec{b} \\
k\vec{x}&= \vec{R}\vec{A}\vec{R}\vec{b}
\end{align}
Finally divide the LHS and RHS by constant k in order to get the value of x.
So, by putting the values of $\vec{R}, \vec{A}$ and $\vec{b}$ in equation \ref{eq:solutions/line_plane/231} we can easily find out the value of x as follows:
